What is Home automation company?

A home automation company specializes in integrating technology into residential spaces to create smart homes. They design and install systems that allow homeowners to control and automate various aspects of their home, such as lighting, heating, cooling, security, entertainment, and more, through devices like smartphones or voice assistants. These companies help enhance convenience, energy efficiency, and security by connecting devices and creating seamless automation tailored to the homeowner's needs.
